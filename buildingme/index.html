How many workout plans do you want to create?

1 plan means you will use the same workout routine everyday. 2 plans mean you will use two different workout routines, interchangeably. If you plan on working out 5 times per week and you choose 5 plans, that means everyday you will have a different workout plan, which you'll be repeating once a week.
